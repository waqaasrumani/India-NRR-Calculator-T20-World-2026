# India-NRR-Calculator-T20-World-2026
A small calculator made to calculate the NRR of India after their match against Zimbabwe in the T20 World cup 2026. Users need to input for different scenarios i.e. India's batting first or second and then accordingly input the detail required. This will give them the estimate of what India's NRR will after their match against Zimbabwe. Also it matters only if India win and the SA vs WI a day prior results in a WI victory.
